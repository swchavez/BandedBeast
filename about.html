It all began with the love of a dog.

After rescuing Holly while working at the County Seat Animal Hospital, we quickly became best friends. We were inseparable as she would follow me around and even came with me on my hunting trips. I have always enjoyed being out in nature and like my companion, Holly, I have always appreciated the highest of quality. That is why I began handcrafting my own hunting accessories with Bridle Leather because of its durable nature which allows the leather to remain of high quality for a long time. Quality and satisfaction is my main focus, but my goal in handcrafting each item is to offer a unique and classy product that every hunter will be proud to show off.
