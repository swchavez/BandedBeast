Banded Beast was established in 2020 in North Mississippi and specializes in crafting high-quality leather goods. From duck straps and hats with leather patches to wallets, belts, and sunglass cases, every product is individually handmade using premium bridle leather from the United States. Whether it's a custom piece or one of our signature items, our focus is on delivering unmatched craftsmanship.

It all began with the love of a dog.

After rescuing Holly while working at the County Seat Animal Hospital, we quickly became best friends. We were inseparable as she would follow me around and even came with me on my hunting trips.

Holly and I have always appreciated the highest of quality. That is why I began hand crafting my own leather accessories with Bridle Leather because of its hundreds of years of artisan craftsmanship, its durable nature, and longitivtiy. Quality and satisfaction is my main focus but my goal in handcrafting each item is to offer a unique and classy product that will quickly become a favorite.
